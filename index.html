HOW HOLO-BATTLES WORK
Complete breakdown of combat, rules, and where to find arenas
BATTLE FORMAT

Holo-Battles are 3v3 turn-based matches. Only one creature is active at a time per side. The battle ends when one team has all 3 creatures knocked out.

Because these are holographic simulations, your pets take no real damage — every creature is fully restored after the match ends.

YOUR TURN OPTIONS

Use an ability — Each creature has 5 unique moves. Stronger moves have cooldowns so you can't spam them. Pick wisely.

Swap creature — You can switch to a different teammate mid-battle. Use this to exploit type matchups against the enemy.

Surrender — Forfeit the match if it's lost.

TURN ORDER & COMBAT MECHANICS

The creature with the highest Agility always acts first each round.

Creatures can randomly dodge incoming attacks, land critical hits, or perform bonus moves — adding unpredictability to every fight.

Before the match starts you choose the order of your 3 creatures. You can also inspect the opponent's team to plan your counter strategy.

BATTLE FLOW SUMMARY
Inspect opponent's team and pick your 3 creatures + order
Highest Agility creature goes first
Select one of your 5 abilities (watch cooldowns!)
Swap if affinity matchup is bad
Last team standing wins

Holo-Arena tables are scattered across the galaxy

The main hub. Find Iteration: Oceanus here — your tutorial guide and the source of the daily challenge (same puzzle for all players worldwide).
▶ Also where multiplayer PvP battles happen — best place to find real opponents

Look near the mission and trade terminals. Space station NPCs are system champions — harder than planetary NPCs but give better rewards.
▶ Best for farming nanites once your team is strong

Found in seating areas where NPCs hang out. These are easier opponents — good for leveling up new creatures or warming up.

You can construct a Xeno Arena structure at your base. Open it to other travellers, host tournaments, or challenge players you meet while exploring.

DAILY CHALLENGE TIP: Oceanus on the Space Anomaly offers a fixed daily battle — the same for every player. The community discusses optimal lineups online, so check Reddit or Discord if you're stuck.
AFFINITY SYSTEM
Every creature's element — determined by its home planet biome
KEY RULE: A creature's affinity is set by the biome of the planet it evolved on. To get a specific affinity, find and tame creatures from the matching biome. Before each fight, inspect the opponent's team — then deploy your best counter.

THE TWO AFFINITY LOOPS
There are two separate rock-paper-scissors chains
LOOP 1 — ELEMENTAL CHAIN
🔥 Fire → ❄ Frost → 🏜 Desert → ☢ Radioactive → 🔥 Fire

Arrow means "beats". Fire beats Frost, Frost beats Desert, Desert beats Radioactive, Radioactive beats Fire.

LOOP 2 — ORGANIC/TECH CHAIN
☠ Toxic → 🌿 Tropical → ⚙ Mech → 🌀 Anomalous → ⚙ Mech

Toxic beats Tropical, Tropical beats Mechanical, Mechanical beats Anomalous... but Anomalous also beats Mechanical — this loop has a twist!

PRO TIP — CHANGE AFFINITY MOVE: One of the rarest and most powerful moves in the game forces the enemy creature to change its affinity. If you're facing a strong counter to your team, this move can completely flip the matchup mid-battle.
STATS & TIER SYSTEM
Understanding your creature's combat numbers and how to improve them
⚔ COMBAT EFFECTIVENESS
Raw damage outputOffensive

Controls how hard your offensive abilities hit. High combat effectiveness makes your attacks, DoTs, and combo moves deal more damage. Prioritize this if you want to play aggressively and KO enemies before they can act.

⚡ AGILITY
Turn order + evasionSpeed

Does two things: determines who goes first (highest Agility acts first), and adds a chance for incoming attacks to miss entirely. A fast creature can dominate a fight by acting first every round and occasionally dodging hits.

❤ HEALTH
Damage capacitySurvival

How much punishment the hologram can take before it's knocked out and you're forced to send in the next creature. High health creatures can act as tanks — staying on the field longer and burning enemy cooldowns. Physical size also affects health — larger creatures tend to be more durable.

🧠 PERSONALITY & PHYSICAL TRAITS

A creature's personality influences how it behaves in battle — some traits may cause bonus aggression or defensive instincts.

Physical size affects both durability and how easy the creature is to target. Bigger = more HP but easier to hit. Smaller = less HP but harder to hit.

CREATURE TIERS
Like ships and multi-tools, arena creatures are ranked C → S
S
S-TIER — LEGENDARY
Peak stats, often found on rare biome planets. Very hard to find in the wild. Worth investing Retroviral Pellets into. Some legendary variants with exceptional stats are extraordinarily rare.
A
A-TIER — EXCELLENT
Strong base stats. Good investment for competitive play. Can beat S-tier with the right affinity counter and good strategy.
B
B-TIER — SOLID
Perfectly viable for most NPC fights. Will struggle against maxed S-tier opponents. Great for learning the system.
C
C-TIER — BEGINNER
Starting point for most tamed creatures. Don't write them off — upgrade with XP mutations and Retroviral Pellets to advance through tiers.
TIER PROGRESSION: You must fully complete one tier before advancing to the next. You can't skip tiers by dumping Retroviral Pellets. Plan your evolution path — identify your main creature first, then spend resources on it.
MORPHOGENETICS — MUTATION SYSTEM
How creatures improve with XP and battle experience
XP MUTATIONS (FREE)

Every battle a creature participates in gives XP. As XP accumulates you unlock genetic mutations that can improve:

• Chance to move first (Agility boost)
• Dodge rate increase
• Overall durability (HP boost)
• Power of all combat abilities

RETROVIRAL PELLETS (FAST TRACK)

Direct gene editing — instantly boost specific stat ratings. Dropped occasionally from battle victories.

⚠ SUMMON YOUR CREATURE FIRST before using a pellet — if you use it before summoning the right creature you waste it!

BREEDING & EGG MODIFIERS
Neural Calibrator items — what to feed eggs to shape personality traits
HOW IT WORKS: At the Egg Sequencer aboard the Space Anomaly, you can feed items via the Neural Calibrator to influence a creature's personality traits. Traits affect battle behavior — Aggression influences offensive decisions, Independence affects how the creature acts on its own, and Helpfulness may affect team-support tendencies.
⚠ DON'T USE RETROVIRAL PELLETS WRONG: Always summon the creature first, then feed it the pellet. If you use it before summoning, it gets wasted on the wrong creature. You won't get another one immediately.
